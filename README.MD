A datapack that makes totems a limited-use item.  
  
This works by making each successive totem take away part of your max health:  
- 0 Totems: 10 Hearts  
- 1 Totem: 9 Hearts  
- 2 Totems: 7 Hearts
- 3 Totems: 4 Hearts
- 4 Totenms: Dead  
  
this makes hardcore actually hard :3 (or at least a bit harder than right now)
